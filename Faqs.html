How can i track my oder
You can track your order by visiting the "Track Order" section on our website and entering your order number and email address. You will also receive a tracking link from the shipping carrier in your confirmation email.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods, including credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), mobile payments like M-Pesa, and online payment platforms such as PayPal.
How can I track my order?
Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number and a link to the courier's tracking page. You can use this information to monitor your order's status in real-time.
What is your return and exchange policy?
We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase, provided the items are unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt. For more information, please visit our Return & Exchange Policy page.
